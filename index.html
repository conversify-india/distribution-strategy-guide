1. Distribution & Strategy

Our core routing system matches client projects with resource capabilities to optimize timeline, accuracy, and translation cost.

STEP 1

Based on Category

Group A: Specialized

(Legal, Medical, Automotive, Technical)

  • No assumptions
  • Terminology must match project requirements
  • Paraphrasing is allowed, but the meaning and terminology must remain unchanged.

Group B: General/Misc

(General, ID/Bank Docs, Certificates)

  • Intake: Email or Physical Docs
  • Zero tolerance for data omissions
  • Share draft with client before final handover
STEP 2

Technical Preparation & OCR Gate

File Conversion Protocol
SENIOR ACTION REQUIRED

Determine if the file can be sent directly to the translator or requires technical extraction to ensure editability. The path forward is determined by your senior's judgment. Examples: InDesign layouts, CAD files, high-resolution scans, or complex deadlines.
